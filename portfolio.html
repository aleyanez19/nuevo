The Decline of Kolmanskop
Namibia

A strange, painful history fills the deserted town of Kolmanskop in Namibia. Once a booming town driven by the diamond rush, it has now been left to the sands of the Namib Desert.

As you walk through these sandy rooms, you can feel the echoes of a once vibrant place, now slowly swallowed by nature.
